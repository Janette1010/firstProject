Things to do!

  • Mac Tabby Cat Cafe

    Come to Mac Tabby drink coffee, teas, local craft beer and also get to play and cuddle with cats!

    Cost:$7-$12, Ages:Family friendly children must be 7+

  • Uptown Mint Museum

    The Mint Museum houses international art and design and is committed to engaging and inspiring all members of our global community

    Cost:$6-$15, Ages:Family friendly

  • Wings Haven

    Located in the heart of Charlotte, North Carolina, Wing Haven provides a natural oasis for reflection and study of its three public gardens. Wing Haven Garden & Bird Sanctuary is rich in Southern horticulture and a habitat for birds and wildlife.

    Cost:$10, Ages:Family friendly!

  • Oh My Soul

    Oh my soul! is nodas first vegan resuartant, Serving burgers,wraps, passtries and much more!

    Cost:$14-$20, Ages:Family friendly!

  • Banh Mi Brothers

    Banh Mi Brothers consists of two brothers and a close friend; who are excited to share their love of this classic, must try, street food with Charlotte. Serving banh mi sandwiches, fires and smoothies!

    Cost:$6.50-$9.50, Ages:Family friendly!

  • Pepperbox Doughnuts

    Pepperbox Doughnuts serves chef driven and vegan friendly yeast-raised doughnuts, handmade in Charlotte, NC.

    Cost:$12.60-$18.90, Ages:Family friendly!
